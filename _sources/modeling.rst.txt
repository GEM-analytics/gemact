(Re)insurance pricing with GemAct
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

GemAct pricing model is based on the collective risk theory. 
In order to price our (re)insurance contracts we model the company aggregate claim cost under the following assumptions.

* :math:`\widetilde{S} \sim g(S)` and :math:`\widetilde{Y_i} \sim f(Y)`
*  Given :math:`\widetilde{N}=n`,  the severity :math:`\widetilde{Y_1},\ldots,\widetilde{Y_n}` is i.i.d and does not depend on :math:`n`.
* :math:`\widetilde{N}` does not depend on :math:`\widetilde{Y_1},\ldots,\widetilde{Y_n}` in any way.

.. math:: \widetilde{S}=\sum_{i=1}^{\widetilde{N}} \widetilde{{Y_i}}
   :label: crm

Denote the pure reinsurance premium as :math:`P= \mathbb{E}\left( S \right)`
