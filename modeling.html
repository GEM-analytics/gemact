Actuarial modeling with GemAct¶

(Re)insurance pricing with GemAct¶

GemAct pricing model is based on the collective risk theory. In order to price our (re)insurance contracts we model the company aggregate claim cost under the following assumptions.

  • \(\widetilde{S} \sim g(S)\) and \(\widetilde{Y_i} \sim f(Y)\)

  • Given \(\widetilde{N}=n\), the severity \(\widetilde{Y_1},\ldots,\widetilde{Y_n}\) is i.i.d and does not depend on \(n\).

  • \(\widetilde{N}\) does not depend on \(\widetilde{Y_1},\ldots,\widetilde{Y_n}\) in any way.

(1)¶\[\widetilde{S}=\sum_{i=1}^{\widetilde{N}} \widetilde{{Y_i}}\]

Denote the pure reinsurance premium as \(P= \mathbb{E}\left( S \right)\)
